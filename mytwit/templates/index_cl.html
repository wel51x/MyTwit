TwitOff is a machine learning app that predicts the Twitter author of any given text. Choose two twitter users, enter some text, and TwitOff will predict which user is most likely to tweet something similar.

When a new user gets added to the database, the word embeddings of their tweets are obtained via Bascilica. When the comparison is run, a logistic regression determines the most likely author.
